  • Clean campsites are safe campsites

    BC Parks requires you to keep your campsite clean or “bare” and all wildlife attractants, such as food and garbage, securely stored whenever you’re away from your site or sleeping – even for 1 minute!

    Follow bare campsite practices

  • Get notified when sites become available

    Looking to make camping plans this summer but missed out on a site? Sign in, set your booking criteria and click the green Notify Me button to receive email alerts for sites that open.

    Save time and set up your alerts now

  • Renewing BC Parks and Recreation

    We are renewing B.C.’s provincial parks and recreation system to help keep trails, campgrounds, and day-use areas safe, reliable, and accessible.

    Learn more about updated camping rates

  • Reservations now available on a 3-month rolling window

    Reservations for campsites, cabins and backcountry reservations are available up to 3 months before your arrival date.
